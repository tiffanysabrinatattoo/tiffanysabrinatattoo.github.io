about

I’m half Swiss, half American, raised in Phoenix Arizona, USA. I moved to Finland in 2020 and now call Helsinki my permanent home. I began my apprenticeship at Flow Tattoo Studio in the summer of 2024.

While I appreciate many forms of art, I am most passionate about creating designs and illustrations that can be enjoyed by many people. I find the most joy by reviving medieval and vintage illustrations, whether that is redesigning an image to be suitable for a tattoo or modernizing an illustration by adding modern elements.

Some of my biggest inspirations in tattooing come from Evie Holmes (@evieholmestattoo), Kelly Rico (@kellyricotattoo), and Jaime Montalvo (@rekka.tattoo), just to name a few. 

When I’m not drawing, I enjoy hobbies such as knitting and playing video games.
